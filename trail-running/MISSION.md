# Mission: Trail running for Brenta 21K

## Why
Learn trail running in service of one concrete goal: finish the XTERRA Dolomiti di Brenta Trail SHORT 21K on 2026-09-05 within the cutoffs. The race is not a flat half marathon: it is about 21 km with 1,250 m D+, a 3h cutoff at Rifugio Croz dell'Altissimo, and long steep climbing/descending segments.

## Success looks like
- Choose when to run and when to power-hike climbs without treating hiking as failure.
- Descend technical trails with controlled, short steps instead of hard braking.
- Read the route profile and translate it into effort, gear, fueling, and pacing decisions.
- Arrive at race day with a simple execution plan: conservative start, controlled climbs, safe descents, no cutoff panic.

## Constraints
- The teaching must stay tied to the Brenta plan in `../trail-brenta-2026/`.
- Lessons should be short enough to review before a training run.
- Technique advice must be conservative: finishing healthy beats chasing pace.
- Use trusted sources and cite them in lessons.

## Out of scope
- Ultramarathon strategy beyond what transfers to a mountainous 21K.
- Maximal downhill racing speed.
- Generic road-running theory unless it directly supports the trail goal.
